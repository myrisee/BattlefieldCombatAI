UFPS versions 2.x shouldn't require any code modifications.  
For UFPS versions 1.x, see the PDF manual.

However, you may need to modify the layermasks on the TSAI bullets, AI Controllers and Cover Nodes.
	Bullets should include the layers that your environment, player and agents are on.
	AI Controllers and Cover Nodes should include the layer your environment is on.

You will also need to attach a TargetScript to your player.
